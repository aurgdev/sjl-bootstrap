Groupage Solutions

With an expanded network through the south, central Europe and Morocco, SJL offers optimized and reliable groupage solutions to meet the requirements and the challenges of consumers. SJL guaranty a secure, fast and cost-efficient service from our pickup origin to the final destination. Groupage and LTL solutions Europe

Milk runs and LTL services to and from Spain to West Europe with cost-efficient and high-performance regular line hauls.

  • Important Network of agents in Europe

  • Regular line hauls from North Spain, Zaragoza & Madrid to France, Belgium, Holland, Germany, Italy and UK.

  • Consolidation hubs in Irun, Paris, Lyon, Brusels and Ripon, Essex (UK).

  • Predefined Transit Times with Regular Departures for LTL solutions

  • 25 daily departures to all over Europe
